The factors of a number are two numbers that multiply to get that number. You might recognise that \(6\) is divisible by 2 and 3. You might also notice that \(2 \times 3 = 6\text{.}\) Since 2 and 3 are separated by multiplication signs, \(2\) and \(3\) are factors of \(6\text{.}\)
